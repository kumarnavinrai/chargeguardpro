Charge Guard

Limitless Power, Effortless Control, Live Free

ChargeGuard is a groundbreaking project that seamlessly bridges the realms of hardware and software for a more efficient and intelligent approach to charging. Comprising both a sophisticated mobile app and cutting-edge IoT hardware, ChargeGuard empowers users to take control of their charging experience. The hardware component allows for precise management of charging cycles by automatically activating or deactivating the power source based on predefined charging levels, ensuring that devices are always optimally charged. In addition to its charging prowess, ChargeGuard extends its capabilities to laptops through specialized software, offering users the ability to monitor and control laptop charging with ease. What's more, the inclusion of a programmable timer function enables the automation of real electricity switches, allowing users to effortlessly manage devices at specific intervals. With ChargeGuard, it's not just about charging; it's about harnessing control, efficiency, and adaptability in the world of device management.
